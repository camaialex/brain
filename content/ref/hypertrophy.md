# Hypertrophy

Cellular adaptation that involves _increase_ in **cell size**.
## Stimuli
- Increased demand
- Hormonal stimulation
## Which Cells?
- Cells with limited replicative capacity ([[permanent cells]], perhaps [[stable cells]])
