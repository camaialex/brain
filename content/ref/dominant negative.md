# Dominant Negative

- When one [[amorphic]] mutation is sufficient to disrupt a normal process
	- Often a [[missense mutation]]
	- Impacts protein folding, multi-subunit enzymes, etc.

## Examples
- [[osteogenesis imperfecta]] Type II
