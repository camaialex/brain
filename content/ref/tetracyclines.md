# Tetracyclines

## Classification
- 
## Pharmacodynamics
- Binds to 30S ribosome, preventing tRNA binding
- Bacteriostatic
- Toxicity to [[bones]] of developing teeth
## Pharmacokinetics
- [[oral]] administration
- **Non-renal** clearance
- Long half-life
## Resistance Mechanisms
- MDR efflux transporters
- Tetracycline blockers
## Examples
- Doxycycline
