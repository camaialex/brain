# Hyperemia

**Increased blood flow** due to active [[arterioles|arteriolar]] dilation.
- Causes [[erythema]], or reddening of nearby tissue
- Causes [[edema]]
## Stimulators of Hyperemia
- [[prostaglandin]]s
- [[histamine]]
- [[NO]]
