# Complete Blood Count
Provides information on the morphology, relative, and absolute amounts of major cell lines in the blood.
- [[erythrocyte|RBC]]s
- [[platelet]]s
- WBCs (differential)
	- [[lymphocyte]]s
		- [[T cell]]s
		- [[B cell]]s
	- [[granulocyte]]s
		- [[neutrophil]]s (bands AND segs)
		- [[eosinophil]]s
		- [[basophil]]s
	- monocytic lineage
		- [[monocyte]]s
		- macrophages usually not present in blood
## Methodology and Interpretation
_Some parameters can be measured both directly and indirectly._
### RBC Parameters
- [[RBC count]]
	- DIRECT: aperture impedance or light scattering
- [[hematocrit]]
- [[hemoglobin concentration]]
	- [[MCH]]
	- [[MCHC]]
- [[MCV]]
	- DIRECT: aperture impedance or light scattering
	- INDIRECT: MCV = Hct / RBC
- [[RDW]]
- RBC precursors
	- Nucleated RBC count
	- [[reticulocyte count]]
### Platelet Parameters
- [[PLT count]]
	- DIRECT: aperture impedance or light scattering
- [[MPV]]
### WBC Parameters
- Absolute lymphocyte count (ALC)
- [[WBC differential]]
## Common Findings
### [[Anemia]]
### [[Immunodeficiency]]
- ALC down
- ANC down in [[neutropenia]]
