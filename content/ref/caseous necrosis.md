# Caseous Necrosis

"Cheese" necrosis
## Etiology
- Formation of a [[granuloma]] and subsequent death of everything inside
- Often caused by [[tuberculosis]] infection or some [[fungus|fungi]]
## Histopathology 
- Central amorphous mass of cell debris
- Inflammatory cell border
