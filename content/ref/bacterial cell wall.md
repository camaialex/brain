# Bacterial Cell Wall

- Provides protection against **osmotic pressure** and outside environment

## Structure
- Made primarily of [[peptidoglycan]]
- **In Gram-positive bacteria**:
	- [[lipoteichoic acid]] and [[teichoic acid]]
- **In Gram-negative bacteria**:
	- [[lipopolysaccharide]]
- **In mycobacteria**:
	- [[mycolic acid]]
