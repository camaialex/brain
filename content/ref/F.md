# Fraction Bioavailable

Bioavailability is measured as the fraction of a drug bioavailable
- Measured in reference to **IV** (assumed 100% bioavailability)
- F = AUC / AUC (IV)

F < 1 for enteral routes due to the [[first-pass effect]]
