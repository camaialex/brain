# DNA Virus

## Characteristics
- Use **DNA** as genomic material
- Replicate in the **nucleus** with few exceptions
	- Poxviruses
- Replication is relatively _high-fidelity_
	- Doesn't evolve rapidly
- Leverage **host DNA polymerase** to replicate
