# ATR (ataxia telangiectasia related)

ATR is a protein involved in detecting **DNA SSBs**.
## Signaling Pathway
1. ATR detects SSB
2. ATR activates [[p53]]
	1. ATR also activates p53 through the [[CHK1]] complex
