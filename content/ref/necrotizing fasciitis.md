# Necrotizing Fasciitis

## Symptoms
- "Flesh-eating disease"
- Fast-spreading, _extremely painful_ erythematous rash
- A medical emergency
## Etiology
- **Type I**: Polymicrobial
- **Type II**: Monomicrobial - [[Streptococcus pyogenes]]
