# Nondisjunction

- Failure of **tetrads** to separate (meiosis I) or **sister chromatids** to separate (meiosis II)

## Disease caused by nondisjunction
- [[Down's syndrome]]
- [[Patau syndrome]]
- [[Edward's syndrome]]
