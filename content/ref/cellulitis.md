# Cellulitis

## Symptoms
- Infection down to the deep [[dermis]]
- _Poorly-demarcated_, hot, red rash
## Etiology
- [[Streptococcus pyogenes]] - Spreading cellulitis
- [[Staphylococcus aureus]] - Localized cellulitis
