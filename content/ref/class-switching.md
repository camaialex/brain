# Class-Switching

Process by which B cells switch the **constant region** of [[immunoglobulin]] heavy chain to fit a specific effector function.
- Requires **costimulation** by [[helper T cell|Th]] cells and [[CD40L]]
## Constant Region Classes
- [[IgM]]
- [[IgG]]
- [[IgD]]
- [[IgA]]
- [[IgE]]
