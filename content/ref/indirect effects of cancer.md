# Indirect Effects of Cancer

- _Hypercoagulative_ state
	- Thrombosis
- _Hypercatabolic_ state
	- Cachexia
- **Paraneoplastic** effects
	- Ectopic hormone expression
	- Hypercalcemia
	- Antibody syndromes
