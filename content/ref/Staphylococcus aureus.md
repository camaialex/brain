# S. aureus
_Staphylococcus aureus_

## Diagnostic
- Cocci
- [[Gram-positive bacteria]]
- **Catalase**: positive
- **Coagulase**: positive
- **Hemolysis**: beta
## Virulence
- Virulence factors:
	- [[Protein A]]
	- [[TSST-1]]
	- [[exfoliative toxins]]
	- [[enterotoxin]]s A-E

## Pathology
- Common infections:
	- Causes _pyogenic_ (pus-forming) infections
	- [[skin]] infections
	- Surgical site
	- [[bone]] - osteomyelitis and septic arthritis
	- [[heart]] - endocarditis
	- [[bacteremia]]
	- [[urogenital epithelium|UTI]]s
	- Food poisoning
- Nosocomial infections:
	- [[lungs]] - [[pneumonia]]
- Neonatal and pediatric infections:
	- [[bullous impetigo]]
	- [[scalded skin syndrome]]
## Therapy
- MRSA: [[cephalosporins]]
- MSSA: [[penicillins]] or [[cephalosporins]]
